Returns & Exchanges Policy

Last updated: February 25, 2026

Shop with Confidence! We offer a hassle-free 30-day return policy. If you're not completely satisfied with your purchase, we're here to help.

30-Day Return Policy

You have 30 calendar days from the date you receive your item to request a return or exchange. To be eligible for a return, your item must be:

  • Unworn and unused
  • In the same condition that you received it
  • In the original packaging with all tags attached
  • Accompanied by proof of purchase (order number or receipt)

Items that do not meet these requirements may not be eligible for a return or exchange.

How to Return an Item

Step 1: Initiate Your Return

Contact our customer service team to start your return:

  • Email: returns@dealheaven.site
  • Phone: (617) 248-9910
  • Include: Your order number, item(s) you wish to return, and reason for return

Step 2: Receive Return Authorization

We'll send you a Return Authorization (RA) number and prepaid return shipping label within 24 hours. For orders over $100, return shipping is FREE!

Step 3: Pack Your Item

  • Place the item in its original packaging if possible
  • Include all tags, accessories, and documentation
  • Print and include the RA number with your return
  • Pack securely to prevent damage during transit

Step 4: Ship Your Return

Attach the prepaid return label to your package and drop it off at any authorized carrier location. We recommend getting a receipt as proof of return.

Return Shipping Costs

✓ Orders Over $100

Return shipping is FREE! We'll provide a prepaid return label.

Orders Under $100

A flat rate of $10 will be deducted from your refund to cover return shipping costs.

Exchanges

Return shipping for exchanges is always FREE, regardless of order value!

Refunds

Once we receive your return, we'll inspect the item and process your refund within 5-7 business days.

Your refund will be issued to the original payment method:

  • Credit/Debit Cards: 5-10 business days (depending on your bank)
  • PayPal: 3-5 business days
  • Cash on Delivery: Store credit or check mailed within 7-10 business days

You'll receive an email confirmation once your refund has been processed. If you don't receive your refund within the expected timeframe, please contact us.

Exchanges

We're happy to exchange your item for a different size, color, or style! The fastest way to get what you want is to:

  1. Return the original item following our return process
  2. Place a new order for the item you want

Priority Exchange Service: Need your exchange quickly? Mention "Priority Exchange" when requesting your return authorization, and we'll ship your new item as soon as we receive your return.

Remember: Exchange shipping is always FREE, even if your original order was under $100!

Non-Returnable Items

For health and hygiene reasons, the following items cannot be returned:

  • Earrings and body jewelry
  • Intimate apparel and swimwear (if hygiene seal is broken)
  • Sale or clearance items marked "Final Sale"
  • Gift cards
  • Items without original tags or packaging
  • Items showing signs of wear or damage

If you receive a damaged or defective item, please contact us immediately for a replacement or refund.

Damaged or Defective Items

We inspect all items before shipping, but if you receive a damaged or defective item:

  1. Contact us within 48 hours of delivery
  2. Provide photos of the damage or defect
  3. Include your order number

We'll immediately send you a replacement or issue a full refund, including original shipping costs. You won't need to return the damaged item unless specifically requested.

Wrong Item Received

If you receive the wrong item, we sincerely apologize! Please contact us immediately:

  • We'll send the correct item right away with expedited shipping
  • You'll receive a prepaid return label for the wrong item
  • No need to wait - your correct item ships immediately!

Late or Missing Refunds

If you haven't received your refund yet, here's what to do:

  1. Check your bank account or credit card statement again
  2. Contact your bank - there may be processing time before a refund is posted
  3. Contact your credit card company - it may take time before your refund is officially posted
  4. If you've done all of this and still haven't received your refund, contact us at hello@dealheaven.site

International Returns

Currently, we only accept returns from orders shipped within the United States. International return policies will be updated as we expand our shipping options.

Gift Returns

Received a gift from Deal Heaven? No problem!

  • Follow the same return process outlined above
  • Provide the gift giver's order number if available
  • Refunds for gifts will be issued as store credit
  • Store credit never expires and can be used on any future purchase
