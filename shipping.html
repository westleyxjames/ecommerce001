Shipping Policy

Last updated: February 25, 2026

Free Shipping

We're pleased to offer FREE standard shipping on all orders over $100 within the continental United States. This is our way of saying thank you for choosing Deal Heaven.

Pro Tip: Add items to your cart to reach $100 and enjoy free shipping!

Shipping Options & Costs

Standard Shipping

  • Cost: $10 (FREE on orders $100+)
  • Delivery Time: 3-5 business days
  • Tracking: Included
  • Coverage: All 50 US states

Express Shipping

  • Cost: $25
  • Delivery Time: 2-3 business days
  • Tracking: Real-time tracking included
  • Coverage: All 50 US states

Overnight Shipping

  • Cost: $45
  • Delivery Time: Next business day
  • Tracking: Real-time tracking included
  • Coverage: Select major cities (check at checkout)
  • Cutoff Time: Orders must be placed by 2 PM EST

Order Processing Time

All orders are processed within 1-2 business days (Monday - Friday, excluding holidays). Orders placed on weekends or holidays will be processed on the next business day.

You will receive a shipping confirmation email with tracking information once your order has been dispatched.

Order Tracking

Once your order ships, you'll receive a confirmation email with a tracking number. You can track your package in real-time using our Order Tracking page.

If you haven't received tracking information within 2 business days of placing your order, please contact us.

International Shipping

Currently, we only ship within the United States (including Alaska and Hawaii). We're working on expanding our international shipping options. Please check back soon or contact us if you have specific international shipping needs.

P.O. Boxes & Military Addresses

P.O. Boxes: We can ship to P.O. Boxes using USPS. Please note that express and overnight shipping options are not available for P.O. Box addresses.

APO/FPO Addresses: We're proud to ship to APO/FPO military addresses. Delivery times may vary, typically 7-14 business days. Standard shipping rates apply, and orders over $100 ship free.

Shipping Restrictions

Due to carrier restrictions, some items may have shipping limitations:

  • Oversized items may require special handling and additional shipping fees
  • We cannot ship to P.O. Boxes for express or overnight services
  • Some remote areas may have extended delivery times

You'll be notified during checkout if any restrictions apply to your order.

Lost or Damaged Packages

All packages are insured during transit. If your package is lost or arrives damaged:

  1. Contact us immediately at (617) 248-9910
  2. Provide your order number and photos of any damage
  3. We'll file a claim with the carrier and send a replacement or refund

For damaged items, please do not discard the packaging as it may be needed for the carrier claim.

Incorrect or Incomplete Address

Please ensure your shipping address is correct and complete before placing your order. We are not responsible for orders shipped to incorrect addresses provided by the customer.

If you realize your address is incorrect after placing an order, contact us immediately. If the package hasn't shipped yet, we can update the address. Once shipped, address changes may not be possible and could result in additional fees.

Holidays & Peak Seasons

During holiday seasons and major sales events, processing and delivery times may be extended due to high order volumes. We recommend:

  • Ordering early if you need items by a specific date
  • Choosing express shipping for time-sensitive orders
  • Checking our website for holiday shipping deadlines
