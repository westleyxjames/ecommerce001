Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to the most common questions about Deal Heaven. Can't find what you're looking for? Contact us and we'll be happy to help!

Orders & Shipping

How do I track my order?
Once your order ships, you'll receive a confirmation email with a tracking number. You can also track your order on our Order Tracking page by entering your order number. Tracking information is typically available within 24 hours of shipment.
When will my order ship?
All orders are processed within 1-2 business days (Monday-Friday, excluding holidays). Orders placed on weekends or holidays will be processed on the next business day. You'll receive a shipping confirmation email once your order has been dispatched.
Do you offer free shipping?
Yes! We offer free standard shipping on all orders over $100 within the continental United States. For orders under $100, standard shipping is $10. See our Shipping Policy for more details.
Can I change my shipping address after placing an order?
If your order hasn't shipped yet, we may be able to update your address. Please contact us immediately at (617) 248-9910. Once an order has shipped, we cannot change the delivery address. In that case, you'll need to contact the carrier directly.
Do you ship internationally?
Currently, we only ship within the United States (including Alaska and Hawaii). We're working on expanding our international shipping options. Please check back soon!

Returns & Exchanges

What is your return policy?
We offer a 30-day return policy from the date you receive your item. Items must be unworn, unused, in original packaging with all tags attached. Return shipping is free on orders over $100. See our Returns & Exchanges page for complete details.
How do I start a return?
Contact our customer service team at returns@dealheaven.site or call (617) 248-9910. Include your order number and the item(s) you wish to return. We'll send you a Return Authorization number and prepaid return label within 24 hours.
How long does it take to get my refund?
Once we receive your return, we'll process your refund within 5-7 business days. Refunds are issued to the original payment method. Credit card refunds typically take 5-10 business days to appear, while PayPal refunds take 3-5 business days.
Can I exchange an item for a different size or color?
Yes! The fastest way is to return the original item and place a new order for what you want. Exchange shipping is always free, even if your original order was under $100. Need it quickly? Mention "Priority Exchange" when requesting your return authorization.

Payment & Security

What payment methods do you accept?
We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, PayPal, and Cash on Delivery (COD). All credit card transactions are processed through secure, encrypted payment gateways to protect your information.
Is it safe to use my credit card on your site?
Absolutely! We use industry-standard SSL encryption to protect your payment information. We never store your complete credit card details on our servers. All transactions are processed through PCI-compliant payment processors.
Do you offer Cash on Delivery (COD)?
Yes! Cash on Delivery is available for all orders within the continental United States. Simply select "Cash on Delivery" at checkout and pay when you receive your package. Please have exact change ready for the delivery person.
Why was my payment declined?
Payment declines can happen for various reasons: insufficient funds, incorrect card details, expired card, or your bank's fraud protection. Try checking your card details, contacting your bank, or using a different payment method. If problems persist, contact us at (617) 248-9910.

Products & Sizing

How do I find my size?
Each product page includes detailed size information in the specifications section. Our sizes are true to standard US sizing. If you're between sizes, we recommend ordering the larger size. Still unsure? Contact us and we'll help you choose the right size!
Are the product photos accurate?
We strive to display colors and details as accurately as possible. However, colors may vary slightly due to different screen settings and lighting. If you're not satisfied with your purchase, our 30-day return policy has you covered!
Do you restock sold-out items?
Popular items are usually restocked! Contact us about a specific item, and we'll let you know if and when it's expected to be back in stock. You can also sign up for restock notifications on product pages.
How do I care for my items?
Care instructions are included on each product page in the specifications section and on the care label attached to your item. Generally, we recommend following the care label instructions to keep your items looking their best. When in doubt, gentle hand washing or dry cleaning is safest for delicate items.

Account & Orders

Do I need an account to place an order?
No, you can checkout as a guest. However, creating an account makes it easier to track your orders, save your shipping address, and get faster checkouts on future purchases.
Can I cancel or modify my order?
Contact us immediately at (617) 248-9910 if you need to cancel or modify your order. If your order hasn't been processed yet, we can make changes. Once an order has shipped, you'll need to return it following our standard return process.
How do I apply a promo code?
You can enter your promo code during checkout in the "Discount Code" field before completing your purchase. Make sure to click "Apply" to see the discount reflected in your order total. Promo codes cannot be applied to orders after they've been placed.

Contact & Support

How can I contact customer service?
We're here to help! Contact us via phone at (617) 248-9910 (Mon-Fri, 9AM-8PM EST), email at hello@dealheaven.site, or visit our Contact page. We respond to all inquiries within 24 hours.
Can I visit your physical store?
Yes! Visit us at 62 Charles Street, Boston, MA 02114. We're open Monday-Friday 10AM-8PM, Saturday 10AM-9PM, and Sunday 11AM-6PM. Come see our latest collections in person!
How do I sign up for your newsletter?
You can sign up for our newsletter at the bottom of any page on our website. We'll send you exclusive offers, style tips, and early access to new collections. Don't worry - we won't spam you, and you can unsubscribe anytime!
